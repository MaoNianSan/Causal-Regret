This archive is a clean rerun-ready source package.

Included:
- EXP1 simulator, learners, delay generator, runner, aggregation, figures,
  self-check, code-check, and paper-result gate.
- Documentation of output schemas, expected tables/figures, trace logging,
  and rerun commands.

Intentionally excluded:
- completed fast/full numerical outputs;
- historical logs and stale manifests;
- executed result notebooks;
- caches, virtual environments, and Git metadata.

The archive content is validated after extraction with `python code_check.py`.
